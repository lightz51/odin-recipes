Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken is a classic Indian dish that is sure to delight your taste buds with its rich and creamy tomato-based sauce, tender chicken pieces, and aromatic spices. This mouth-watering recipe is a must-try for anyone who loves Indian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp ginger and garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the yogurt, lemon juice, chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and turmeric powder. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter and add the chopped onion. Cook until the onion is soft and translucent.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the mixture thickens and the oil separates from the gravy.
  5. Add the marinated chicken and cook for 10-12 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Add the heavy cream and salt to taste. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
