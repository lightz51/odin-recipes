Aloo Gobi

Description

A simple and delicious dish made with potatoes and cauliflower cooked with Indian spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 large potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 small cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and fry until fragrant.
  2. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Cook until the onion is soft and translucent.
  3. Add the chopped tomato, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and cayenne pepper. Cook for a minute.
  4. Add the diced potato and cauliflower florets. Stir to coat in the spice mixture.
  5. Add 1/2 cup of water and salt to taste. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and cooked through.
  6. Remove the cover and cook for another 5-10 minutes or until the liquid has evaporated and the vegetables are lightly browned.
  7. Adjust salt to taste and serve hot with naan or rice.
